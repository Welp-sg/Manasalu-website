Japanese Agriculture & Rice production

"Intoducing Japanese Agriculture to Youths"

The consultancy facilitates educational experiences for visitors interested in rice plantation and agriculture. Guests are welcomed to observe and participate in various aspects of rice cultivation, learning about planting methods and farming techniques. Additionally, the consultancy assists in the sale and import of rice products, connecting producers with international markets. This dual approach of tourism and trade helps sustain local agricultural practices while making quality rice varieties available to a wider audience.
